FAQ's

What types of businesses can benefit from your Audit & Analytics Strategy services?
Our Audit & Analytics Strategy services cater to businesses of all sizes and industries, as effective data management and analytics practices are crucial for informed decision-making and growth across all sectors.
How long does an analytics audit typically take?
The duration of an analytics audit varies depending on the complexity of your data infrastructure and the scope of the project. However, most audits can be completed within a few weeks.
Do you provide implementation support for the recommended analytics strategies?
Yes, our agency offers end-to-end support, including implementation assistance for the recommended strategies, as well as ongoing optimization and performance evaluations.
What kind of analytics tools and platforms are you experienced with?
Our team has expertise in a wide range of analytics tools and platforms, including Google Analytics, Segment, Mixpanel, Looker Studio, BigQuery, Amplitude, and more, ensuring the most suitable solution for your specific needs.
Will your team need access to our current analytics accounts and data?
Yes, to conduct a comprehensive analytics audit and develop an effective strategy, our team will require access to your current analytics accounts and data. We assure you that all data will be handled securely and confidentially.
