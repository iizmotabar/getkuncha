At MarketLytics we want people to work in the way they are most productive and happy. Additionally, we have full trust in everyone on the team. We either work with someone with full trust, or we don\u2019t work together.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\ud83d\udcc5 Your Schedule<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

We all know what schedule works best for us and we support you in adopting the one that suits you the most. Be it starting early and leaving early, starting late and leaving late, starting early and leaving late… We don\u2019t care, everyone should do what makes them happy. In return, we ask that everyone is very responsible and have a strong work ethic, which we all already do.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

There is no bias or preference for any schedule. Every one of us is a unique person and has to do what works best for us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Examples<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Talha<\/strong>
    Talha, one of our Data Associates, just doesn\u2019t do mornings. It\u2019s unlikely you\u2019ll see him in the office before 11 am. He finds that starting at 11 am and working in the evening suits his style better. If something urgent needs to be done, he\u2019ll do it before 11 am, from home.<\/li>
  • Sameen<\/strong>
    Sameen, she is our Senior Data Solutions Specialist, is an early riser. She usually starts working at 8 am and finishes in the early afternoon so she can spend time with her family.<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
