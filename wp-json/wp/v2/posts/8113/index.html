\n What is Dynamic Remarketing?
\n<\/h2>\n

Dynamic remarketing is an advanced remarketing technique which enables you to retarget all previous site visitors with ads containing product or services they viewed. This help you generate more leads by attracting visitors who in the past showed interest in your product but didn\u2019t purchase. <\/span><\/p>\n

There are many advertising platforms which offer dynamic remarketing but here I will focus on dynamic remarketing in google analytics. <\/span><\/p>\n

    Follow the steps below to link your Google Merchant Center with your google    Adwords account. <\/span><\/p>\n

    Step#1: <\/strong>Sign in to your <\/span>Google Merchant Center<\/span><\/a> Account.<\/span><\/p>\n

    Step#2: <\/strong>On the top right side of the window, look for the 3 vertically dotted icon, click <\/span>and select <\/span>Account Linking<\/strong>.<\/span><\/p>\n

    merchant center account linking<\/p><\/div>\n

    Step#3: <\/strong>In the next window, select the adwords tab. Here you will see your adwords accounts list. Look for the adwords account you want to link and click on the <\/span>Link<\/strong> button.<\/span><\/p>\n

    select adwords account<\/p><\/div>\n

    Step#4: <\/strong>Your merchant center is now linked with adwords. You can verify this by <\/span>looking at the status or view in the adwords linked account. <\/span> <\/span><\/p>\n

    \n 4. Create Custom Dimensions to Send Attributes for Vertical to Google Analytics

    Attributes vary according to each industry type, but there is always a single <\/span>required attribute for each category, the rest are optional.  For example if you <\/span>have a retail industry type, the required attribute will be<\/span><\/p>\n

        <\/span>Total Value<\/span><\/p>\n
