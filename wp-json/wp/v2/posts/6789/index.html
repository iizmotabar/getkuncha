Marketlytics is a user data consultancy and advanced analytics provider, helping clients understand their user behavior. We collaborate with a network of clients and partners across 26 countries including F500s, fast-growing startups, and global e-commerce brands.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We are looking to add Data Engineers with at least 1 – 5 years of experience to the team. The role will require working with other Data Engineers to streamline the development, testing, and deployment of data pipelines and products.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/div>\n\n\n\n

What you need to have to join the clan: <\/h3>\n\n\n\n
  • A bachelor’s degree in computer science, engineering, applied math, or a related subject <\/li>
  • Experience with relational SQL and NoSQL databases<\/li>
  • Experience with data pipeline and workflow management tools<\/li>
  • Experience with programming languages\/technologies used in data engineering and data
    science, at least one of Python, R, or Node.JS<\/li>
  • Experience or basic knowledge of working on cloud technologies like GCP or Azure<\/li>
  • Knowledge or prior experience with extracting and creating pipelines for User Behaviour data\/ Marketing Analytics platforms\/ CRMs\/ ERPs would be a plus<\/li>
  • Prior Dashboarding experience with Tableau\/ Powerbi\/ Redash<\/li>
  • Strong communication skills<\/li>
  • Intellectually curious <\/li>
  • Ability to prioritize and manage work independently<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    What you\u2019ll be required to do:<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
    • Create and maintain optimal data pipeline architecture<\/li>
    • Construct documentation for ETL operations, object usage, and data models.<\/li>
    • Assemble large, complex data sets that meet functional\/ non-functional business requirements.<\/li>
    • Build the infrastructure required for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading of data from a wide variety of data sources using SQL and Cloud Native \u2018big data\u2019 technologies.<\/li>
    • Work with data and analytics experts to strive for greater functionality in our data systems.<\/li>
    • Designing and building production-grade data pipelines from ingestion to consumption within a hybrid big data architecture, using Python<\/li>
    • Performing detailed assessments of current state data platforms and creating an appropriate transition path to Azure\/ GCP<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      The benefits you\u2019ll get from being part of the family: <\/h3>\n\n\n\n
      • Compensation strongly tied to performance (Base + Bonus)<\/li>
      • Flexitime <\/li>
      • Paid Vacations<\/li>
      • Health Coverage<\/li>
      • Mentoring <\/li>
      • Learning & Growth Opportunities <\/li>
      • Potentially for right player opportunity to be a co-founder in products we build as well as lead technical teams<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
