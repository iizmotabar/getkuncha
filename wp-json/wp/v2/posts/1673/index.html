If your GTM account is messed up with so many accounts it may be possible that you have deleted a different account mistakenly. It can also be possible that you see a GTM account missing. So there may be two possible cases<\/span><\/p>\n

    \n
  1. Your access has been revoked<\/span><\/li>\n
  2. GTM account was deleted<\/span><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    If your access has been revoked you can ask your client to give access to the specific account. <\/span><\/p>\n

    In case if your GTM account has been deleted it can not be restored and you will have to create a new container and redo all the things again.<\/span><\/p>\n
