Google recently rolled out new features in Google Tag Manager. These features include native YouTube<\/a> and Scroll Tracking<\/a> triggers and variables. If you have noticed one other hidden feature, that enables you to pause any tag within the current workspace.<\/p>\n

Yes, you can pause the tag!<\/p>\n

Follow the simple steps below to pause any tag.<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. Open tags section within your GTM account<\/li>\n
  2. Click on the tag that you want to pause.<\/li>\n
  3. When it opens, click on the three vertical dots from top right<\/li>\n
  4. Click on the pause option<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    <\/p>\n

    To unpause this tag, follow the same steps and click on the unpause button.<\/p>\n
