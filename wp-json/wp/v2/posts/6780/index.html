Marketlytics is a user data consultancy and advanced analytics provider, helping clients understand their user behavior. We collaborate with a network of clients and partners across 26 countries including F500s, fast-growing startups, and global e-commerce brands.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We are looking to add Azure \/ PowerBI Consultant to the team. The role will require working with Software Developers and Data Engineers to streamline the development, testing, and deployment of data pipelines and products.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What you need to have to join the clan: <\/h3>\n\n\n\n
  • At least a Bachelor\u2019s Degree in Computer Science or related discipline. Self-taught individuals with a similar level of knowledge are also welcome<\/li>
  • Expertise with Microsoft Azure Suite of Storage Solutions<\/li>
  • At least 1.5 years of experience with Power BI (focused on optimization of speed\/ DAX)<\/li>
  • At least 2 years of experience working with SQL<\/li>
  • Prior experience building & maintaining data-warehouses using ETL (ideally with Azure Analysis Services)<\/li>
  • Experience in Python would be an added advantage<\/li>
  • Familiarity with Cloud Native Stacks (AWS, Azure or Google Cloud, Kubernetes \/ Docker)<\/li>
  • Experience with Data Mining <\/li>
  • Knowledge or prior experience with Google Analytics \/ Digital Analytics would be a plus<\/li>
  • Analytics and Problem-Solving skills<\/li>
  • Strong Skills in Statistics and Reporting <\/li>
  • Strong communication skills<\/li>
  • Intellectually curious <\/li>
  • Ability to prioritize and manage work independently<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    What you\u2019ll be required to do:<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
    • Strategize, develop and test DWH\/ BI Solutions <\/li>
    • Data pipeline orchestration for collection and analysis using Azure Cloud Stack<\/li>
    • Work on data modelling using SQL with emphasis on data management practices for solutions.<\/li>
    • Create meaningful and impact oriented dashboards using Power BI\/ Tableau<\/li>
    • Design solutions that are scalable, optimized for speed and performance<\/li>
    • Research and explore new tools for pipeline orchestration, analysis and data governance<\/li>
    • Manage, mentor and oversee project execution with team members<\/li>
    • Translate business needs into technical specifications<\/li>
    • Collaborate on problem resolution, team decisions and project planning<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
