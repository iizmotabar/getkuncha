You might be running email campaigns to re-engage users who stopped using your app. It will be difficult to figure out user behavior and targeting those specific people with a single email. Let\u2019s say you are using Google Analytics, but it has no such feature to send emails or messages.<\/p>\n

Why Mixpanel?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Mixpanel is a powerful yet advanced web and mobile analytics tool. It focuses more on user actions rather than just pageviews. It is best suitable for web & mobile applications, SaaS products, and large businesses.<\/p>\n

The beauty of Mixpanel is its core focus on event tracking. It empowers you to make decisions based on what visitors did when they landed on your product and not which page they viewed.<\/p>\n

Another very powerful feature Mixpanel supports is people tracking. You can identify an anonymous visitor and track his\/her actions.<\/p>\n

Using Mixpanel, you can find answers to few of the important questions like:<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. What a visitor did when he\/she first landed on your product?<\/li>\n
  2. Which action does a visitor take frequently?<\/li>\n
  3. Where do people drop off the purchase funnel?<\/li>\n
  4. How often do people use my app?<\/li>\n
  5. How many people subscribe to my app each day?<\/li>\n
  6. and much more\u2026<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    Getting started with Mixpanel<\/a> is easy. You can sign up for a FREE subscription to get your hands dirty with Mixpanel.<\/p>\n

    Intro to Mixpanel Messages<\/strong><\/h2>\n

    Messages in Mixpanel is an awesome feature to re-engage people with your product. To do this, you will have to first identify each user with people tracking<\/a>.<\/p>\n

    You can re-engage people based on their actions, and not just who they are. For example, you can send a coupon code to people who have purchased a product with your app more than 5 times in the last 30 days.<\/p>\n

    How to setup Mixpanel Messages?<\/strong><\/h3>\n

    Messages can only be sent to users who have people profiles. Under a FREE plan, you can send up to 1000 messages per month. For more details about message limits, click here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

    Step1: Compose Message<\/strong><\/p>\n

    To send a message in Mixpanel, navigate to messages from the left panel.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Click create message<\/strong> drop down and select message.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Enter message name and your message<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Step2: Target Users<\/strong><\/p>\n

    The second step is to choose who you want to target. You can choose between people and events to set the condition.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Step3: Schedule Delivery<\/strong><\/p>\n

    The final step is to set the time to send the message. You can send this asp or schedule it for a later time.<\/p>\n

    The best benefit of scheduling message is you can send according to user\u2019s timezone.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Did you achieve your target?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

    Every message you send should have a purpose. For example, a message containing a coupon code should improve lost customer engagement and increase in paying customers.<\/p>\n

    Based on the condition we set in the previous step, we can now check Order Button Click event to see if it has increased after the message has been sent.<\/p>\n
