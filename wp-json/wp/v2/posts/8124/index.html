What are segments?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

A segment in google analytics is a subset of your data. It can be the data about a single country or about single traffic source. You can use segment to isolate a subset of data to understand the major changes that affect your data. <\/span><\/p>\n

Custom segments can be added to any reports in google analytics. You can apply up to 4 segments at a time. All users segment is selected by default to display all data without applying filters.<\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

\n How to add segments in google analytics?
\n<\/h2>\n

To add segments in google analytics click on the add segment button in the reporting tab.<\/span><\/p>\n

add segment<\/p><\/div>\n

Next, from the list of segments, click on the segments which you want to apply and click <\/span>apply<\/strong> button.<\/span><\/p>\n

segments list<\/p><\/div>\n

Once you apply segments, you will see the filtered data based on the segments.<\/span><\/p>\n

active segments<\/p><\/div>\n

In-order to add different segments click on any segment to open popup, un-select the segment you want to remove and then select required segment and click apply.<\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

\n How to Remove Segments?
\n<\/h2>\n

You can remove segment by either unselecting it from popup or selecting \u2018Remove\u2019 from segment drop-down menu that appears by clicking arrow <\/span> on corner of segment button. <\/span><\/p>\n

remove segments<\/p><\/div>\n

\n How to share google analytics segments?
\n<\/h2>\n

Follow the steps below to share google analytics segments.<\/span><\/p>\n

step#1: <\/strong>click on the any of the segment to display the list of segments. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#2:<\/strong> from the segments list, click on the action drop-down menu and select share<\/span><\/p>\n

share segments<\/p><\/div>\n

Copy the generated url and send to anyone you want to share with. <\/span><\/p>\n

<\/span><\/p>\n

\n How to import segments in google analytics?
\n<\/h2>\n

Follow the steps below to import segments.<\/span><\/p>\n

step#1: <\/strong>sign in to your analytics account. <\/span><\/p>\n

step#2: <\/strong>Click on the segment link which you want to import. <\/span><\/p>\n

step#3: <\/strong>select the view in which you want to import and click create button.<\/span><\/p>\n

select view<\/p><\/div>\n
