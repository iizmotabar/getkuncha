What are Custom Reports?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Custom reports in google analytics are the reports that you create. You can do this by selecting the required dimensions and metrics and view them the way you want.<\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

\n How to Create Custom Reports in Google Analytics?
\n<\/h2>\n

Follow the steps below to create custom reports in google analytics.<\/p>\n

Step#1: <\/strong>Login to your <\/span>analytics account<\/strong>.<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#2:<\/strong> Open the specific view in which you want to create custom reports.<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#3: <\/strong>Select the \u2018<\/span>Customization<\/strong>\u2019 tag from the top menu.<\/span><\/p>\n

access custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

Step#4: <\/strong>Click on the create \u2018<\/span>New Custom Report<\/strong>\u2019 button.<\/span><\/p>\n

create custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

Step#5: <\/strong>Enter the title for your custom report. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#6: <\/strong>Enter the name of your reporting tab, select the report type, define dimensions and metrics.<\/span><\/p>\n

setting up custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

Note: adding additional report tabs are optional but every report must contain at least one reporting tab. <\/strong><\/p>\n

Step#6:<\/strong> Filters are optional but this is added to restrict the data for specific dimensions. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#7: <\/strong>This is again optional step. Select from the view options where you want this report to appear. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#8: <\/strong>Click <\/span>Save<\/strong> and you are done. <\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

How to Customize Custom Reports?<\/h2>\n

\n How to access custom reports?
\n<\/h3>\n

To access custom reports select Customization tab from the top menu. <\/span><\/p>\n

access custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

If you have already created any custom reports you will see them listed on the left panel. <\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

\n How to categorize custom reports?
\n<\/h3>\n

Click on the \u2018customization\u2019 tab from the top navigation menu in your analytics account. <\/span><\/p>\n

Now, click on the \u2018<\/span>New Category\u2019<\/strong> button to create a category for your custom reports. <\/span><\/p>\n

create category custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

\n How to edit custom reports?
\n<\/h3>\n

Step#1:<\/strong> To edit custom reports in google analytics, open the report which you want to edit. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#2: <\/strong>Inside the reports, look at the top nav-bar and click on the edit option. <\/span><\/p>\n

edit custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

Step#3: <\/strong>Inside you can change dimensions, metrics or create a new report tab. Click \u2018<\/span>save button<\/strong>\u2019 to save new changes.<\/span><\/p>\n

From the above menu, you can send these reports via email, export in different formats and add this to your dashboard. <\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

\n How to share custom reports?
\n<\/h3>\n

To share custom reports in google analytics follow the below steps:<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#1: <\/strong>select the customization tab to view the list of custom reports.<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#2:<\/strong> Open the specific report which you want to share.<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#3:<\/strong> Add this report to your dashboard.<\/span><\/p>\n

add custom reports to dashboard<\/p><\/div>\n

Step#3: <\/strong>Once you added the report to dashboard, you will see this custom report in your dashboard. Now, from the dashboard click on the share button on the top navigation menu<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#3: <\/strong>From the share button drop-down menu, click on \u2018<\/span>share template link<\/strong>\u2019.<\/span><\/p>\n

share custom reports<\/p><\/div>\n

Step#4: <\/strong>Copy the generated link and send to the person you want to share with.<\/span><\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

\n How to Import Custom Reports?
\n<\/h3>\n

Follow the below steps to import custom reports in google analytics. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#1: <\/strong>Sign In to your analytics account.<\/span><\/p>\n

Step#2:<\/strong> Make sure you have the view level access to import this report. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#3: <\/strong>Click on the custom report link which you want to import. <\/span><\/p>\n

Step#4: <\/strong>A new window will appear to select the view in which you want to import.<\/span><\/p>\n

select view to import custom report<\/p><\/div>\n

Step#5: <\/strong>Select specific view and and click on create button.<\/span><\/p>\n

Once this is done, you will see this report in your private dashboard.<\/span><\/p>\n
