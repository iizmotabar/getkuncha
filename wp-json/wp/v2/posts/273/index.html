Optimize or Optimize 360?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Before diving into the features and benefits of Google Optimize, let\u2019s first decide what type of business you have and which tool will be most beneficial.<\/p>\n

Google Optimize and Google Optimize 360 are two different versions of the testing solution. Google Optimize is a free version and is specially developed for small to medium-sized businesses and Optimize 360 is a premium tool developed for larger businesses with complex testings.<\/p>\n

If you are a newbie, exploring testing techniques with your website I would strongly recommend going with Optimize rather than the alternative. It covers all the basic features you\u2019d need for testing and requires a low learning curve.<\/p>\n

Benefits of Using Google Optimize<\/strong><\/h2>\n

If your website\u2019s testing tool gathers data from multiple sources, which are important to your business, chances are you\u2019d end up with inaccurate results. If you have integrated google analytics on your website, then your main data source would be Google Analytics. Google Optimize is natively integrated with Google Analytics, which makes it easier to collect data from a single source to test the weak areas of your website.<\/p>\n

Google Optimize is the easiest testing tool so far hence allowing users with minimal testing and control knowledge to do the job effortlessly. . You can easily drive site experiments using a user friendly environment offered by google optimize.<\/p>\n

With Google Optimize targeting features you can test your content for each user or group of users, thus delivering and fulfilling the needs of multiple types of users.<\/p>\n

Moreover, Google Optimize is easy to integrate with your website. You only need to insert a line of code to your existing google analytics tracking code.<\/p>\n

Integrating Google Optimize Via Google Tag Manager<\/strong><\/h2>\n

If you are already using Google Tag Manager, you don\u2019t have to worry about installing Google Optimize. It\u2019s more easy to integrate Optimize with Tag Manager then Google Analytics.<\/p>\n

Below is a step by step guide to getting started with Optimize and integrating with Google Tag Manager.<\/p>\n

Sign up for Google Optimize account<\/strong><\/h3>\n

1). Click https:\/\/www.google.com\/analytics\/optimize\/<\/a> and sign up<\/p>\n

2). If you are already signed into your google account, you will be directed to the welcome screen. Click on the get started button to continue.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

3). Next, fill the email subscription form and click accept agreement on the next few screens.<\/p>\n

4). You have created your Optimize account and container. The next step is to connect the container with google analytics specific property. From the right menu, click on link to Google Analytics tab, click link property button and choose the specific property and views.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n
