    How to Identify Users<\/strong><\/h3>\n

    One of the Kissmetrics core features is people tracking. It has the ability to identify your website visitors which is not possible in Google Analytics. Unless you don\u2019t specify how Kissmetrics will identify users, it will treat them as anonymous users by default.<\/p>\n

    The JavaScript code you added in the first step is only capable of assigning an anonymous ID to each visitor on your site. You can identify users by their email, account ID etc.<\/p>\n

    Whenever a visitor fills out a form on your site, you can use use the email from that form to uniquely identify that person.<\/p>\n

    There are two methods for identifying users:<\/p>\n

      \n
    1. Identify users automatically using JavaScript Library<\/a><\/li>\n
    2. Identify users manually with APIs<\/a><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

      Population<\/strong><\/h2>\n

      Population in Kissmetrics is a good feature for creating a segment of people who perform similar actions or behavior on your site. You can group people based on their behavior to create a population that is similar to a segment in Google Analytics.<\/p>\n

      How to Create a Population<\/strong><\/h3>\n

      To create a population, click on the \u201cpopulations\u201d tab from the top navigation menu.<\/p>\n

      <\/p>\n

      Click on the New Population to create your first population<\/p>\n

      <\/p>\n

      Set your condition and then click the view population button.<\/p>\n

      <\/p>\n

      Save this population if you want to monitor this frequently.<\/p>\n

      You can learn more about the best use of Kissmetrics population here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

      Activity Report<\/strong><\/h2>\n

      The activity report in Kissmetrics is a quick way to visualize your events and see which segments are contributing to your growth\/decline of your site.<\/p>\n

      To create an activity report, click on the \u201cReports\u201d tab from the top navigation.<\/p>\n

        \n
      1. Click activity reports<\/li>\n
      2. Click create activity report<\/li>\n
      3. Select an event<\/li>\n
      4. Click on the \u201cRun Report\u201d button<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

        <\/p>\n

        <\/h2>\n

        Campaign<\/strong><\/h2>\n

        Campaign is another important feature in Kissmetrics that allows you to get in touch with your users via email based on their behavior.<\/p>\n

        Every Kissmetrics campaign consists of a message and a goal.<\/p>\n

        You can have any number of messages in a campaign to deliver what you want to convey to people.<\/p>\n

        The goal would be what you want the person to do after reading your message. If he\/she takes the relevant action then your goal is complete. Once you start the campaign, Kissmetrics track your goals against the message and determine the goal conversion rate.<\/p>\n

        To further read the details about the Kissmetrics campaign, click here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

        Funnel Report – Track how People Navigate through your Site<\/strong><\/h2>\n

        By creating a funnel, you can determine the number of people purchasing a product by following the steps you have mentioned.<\/p>\n

        To create a funnel, create a new report under the “Reports” tab. The funnel steps include the events. For example, a purchase funnel would include<\/p>\n

        View product >> add to cart >> checkout >> purchase<\/strong><\/p>\n

        After creating a funnel, click on the run report button to see the funnel report.<\/p>\n

        <\/p>\n

        If you have created this funnel for the first time and need to see it frequently, you will have to save this as a report.<\/p>\n
