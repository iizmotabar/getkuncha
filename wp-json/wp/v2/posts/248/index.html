Managing, integrating and transferring live user data to several tools and warehouses at the same time all the while maintaining its integrity, requires a lot of coding and remodeling efforts. There has to be a simpler way to do it, so that the process involved not only minimizes the overall size of your data but also makes it more comprehensible.<\/p>\n

Here is where Segment<\/a> comes in. It can track your data and send it to multiple tools in real time but what about your old data? The historical user data that was collected before integrating the said Segment? Nonetheless, the previous data is equally essential for accurate reporting as the new one and you would never like to put into the trash because it can help develop future plans.<\/p>\n

For this purpose we use a Segment CSV Importer.<\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

Segment CSV Importer:<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Segment CSV Importer allows you to import all of your historic data in the form of a CSV file to Segment in a matter of seconds. All you need to do is upload your CSV file to Segment CSV Importer and it will send your data to Segment which in turn sends it to all your integrations.<\/p>\n

\u00a0<\/p>\n

How does it work?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Segment CSV Importer upon loading asks you to enter your segment write key and upload your CSV file.<\/p>\n

To open the app please visit: https:\/\/segmentcsvimporter.herokuapp.com\/<\/a> <\/p>\n

Where to find Write key?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

The write key allows our app to connect with your workspace.A workspace is a bunch of sources, linked together in a way that they are controllable under the same spectrum.  You can think of write key as a unique identifier that performs two basic operations:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Inform the segment about the source that generated or produced the data<\/li>\n
  • Where that data should be implied to and which integrations (business tools and apps interlinked via a segment) and workspaces should receive it<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    3.         Select your desired workspace.It will take you to your sources.<\/p>\n

    4.         Click on the required source.<\/p>\n

    5.         Click settings.<\/p>\n

    CSV File Specifications:<\/strong><\/h2>\n

    Now your CSV file should have some mandatory fields for the app to detect your action you want to perform with your data. Segment supports 3 import actions.<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Identify (Who is the customer?)<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Track (What are they doing?)<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Alias (What was their past identity?)<\/p>\n

    For more Detail Information please visit: https:\/\/segment.com\/docs\/spec<\/a><\/p>\n

    So your CSV should have following fields:<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Action (above mentioned)<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             User ID<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Anonymous ID<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Timestamp (yyyy-MM-dd’T’HH:mm:ss.SSS’Z’) details<\/p>\n

                   ( https:\/\/www.w3.org\/TR\/NOTE-datetime)<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Integrations (integration you want to send data)<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Event<\/p>\n

    \u00b7             Properties\/traits<\/p>\n
