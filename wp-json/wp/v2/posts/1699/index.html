Google Analytics is a very useful and profitable tool if used wisely and understand how it works. It has many features which are responsible for collecting data from your site, app or any digitally connected device, processes that data and outputs the reports. <\/span><\/p>\n

Why you need to track time of day?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Sometimes it’s most important to understand google analytics traffic by hour. This way you will be able to see during which hours your site shows the highest traffic. By using google analytics hourly traffic reports you can set your marketing strategy to reach out more traffic. <\/span><\/p>\n

How to track traffic by hour in Google Analytics?<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Google Analytics does not contain a built-in feature that will show traffic according to the hours of the day. There are two methods of doing this in GA, one is to <\/span>create custom report<\/span><\/a> by yourself and secondly importing custom reports from analytics solution gallery. Here I will show you both methods of doing this:<\/span><\/p>\n

Create Custom Reports<\/strong><\/h3>\n

In order to create a custom report sign into your GA account and select customization tab.<\/span><\/p>\n

create custom report<\/p><\/div>\n

Next, enter the title for custom report.<\/span><\/p>\n

Enter a name for the report tab<\/span><\/p>\n

Select type as \u2018Flat Table\u2019<\/span><\/p>\n

Dimension as \u2018Hour\u2019<\/span><\/p>\n

Metrics as \u2018pageviews\u2019, \u2018pages\/session\u2019,\u2019Avg.session duration\u2019, and \u2018Bounce Rate\u2019 in my case but you can select other metrics according to your need.<\/span><\/p>\n

And click save. <\/span><\/p>\n

Your custom report will look something similar to the below screenshot.<\/span><\/p>\n

hour of the day<\/p><\/div>\n

Importing Custom Reports from Analytics Solution Gallery<\/strong><\/h3>\n

If you are not comfortable with using google analytics or new to GA the simplest way is to import custom report from solution gallery, where other members have created and posted for your use. <\/span><\/p>\n
