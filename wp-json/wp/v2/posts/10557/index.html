Marketlytics is a user data consultancy and advanced analytics provider, helping clients understand their user behavior. We collaborate with a network of clients and partners across 26 countries including F500s, fast-growing startups, and global e-commerce brands.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We are looking to add Data Consultant – Client Strategy to the team. The role will require working with a team of Data Engineers, Analysts, and Scientists to streamline the development, testing, and deployment of data pipelines and products.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What you need to have to join the clan: <\/h3>\n\n\n\n
  • At least a Bachelor\u2019s Degree in Computer Science or related discipline. Self-taught individuals with a similar level of knowledge are also welcome<\/li>
  • A clear understanding of Data, Data importing, Data Cleaning, and Manipulation <\/li>
  • Hands-on experience with Data Analytics<\/li>
  • Strong analytical skills<\/li>
  • Ability to perform root cause analysis on data and processes to answer specific questions and identify opportunities for improvement <\/li>
  • Experience with Project Management and Leading<\/li>
  • Experience with Client Communication <\/li>
  • Experience with Requirement Gathering and Documentation<\/li>
  • Quick adaptability to new technology and the ability to research troubleshooting techniques and best practices.<\/li>
  • Interest in building end-to-end solutions that leverage strong system designs principles to build scalable solutions<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    At least one of:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    • 2 years of experience working with SQL or R, Python<\/li>
    • 2 years of experience building and optimizing data architectures, data pipelines, and datasets.<\/li>
    • Familiarity with Cloud Native Stacks (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud)<\/li>
    • Experience with Data Mining & Analysis<\/li>
    • Experience with working with BigQuery, Snowflake.<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      <\/p>\n\n\n\n

      What you\u2019ll be required to do:<\/h3>\n\n\n\n
      • Depending on the project, you will be paired with a Technical Lead and you will be responsible for deliverables ensuring timely delivery and project success from the technical point of view.<\/li>
      • You will be deployed on a number of engagements simultaneously. Where you will be expected to double as a Technical Architect of at least one engagement while being Consultant Data Strategy on others. <\/li>
      • You will build and handle relationships with clients, and teammates. You will be the point of contact for the client. <\/li>
      • You will strategize, scope, and translate project requirements from the client into clear functional specifications and work with the team to transfer them into solutions. <\/li>
      • You will be the owner of project documentation such as scope, sprint doc, WBS, and tickets.<\/li>
      • Be able to do the first level of r&d and technical research to support the client and technical team in making the right choices <\/li>
      • You will track and update project status to management and be the knowledge point for the project.<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
