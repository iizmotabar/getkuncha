Hi! We\u2019re welcoming Data & Development Interns at Markelytics! <\/strong><\/p>\n

MarketLytics is a Google Analytics Certified partner company. We work with some of the coolest businesses out there (you\u2019ve probably visited at least a couple of them over the past year, and we\u2019re talking hotels, mobile apps, maybe even your favorite news & beauty brands to name a few.)<\/p>\n

If you\u2019re into data and would love to get some incredible hands-on training in data engineering and analysis, we think you\u2019d be an awesome fit for our internship program. <\/p>\n

Program Details<\/strong><\/p>\n

Our internship program is a 2.5 month-long, intensive training opportunity, based in our Karachi office. We make sure that interns are fully embedded in teams and encouraged to independently execute solutions supported by experienced mentors. The key areas we focus on are:<\/p>\n

– Hands-on training on how to plan and instrument data pipelines<\/p>\n

– Analysis of data using R & communicating findings<\/p>\n

– Identifying and executing on opportunities to improve a team\u2019s workflow using data & programming<\/p>\n

And just so you can get a feel of the kind of work experience you can expect here, here are a few of the things our interns did last year: <\/p>\n

Kaptaan in The Light of Data: Exploratory and Sentiment Analysis Part 1<\/span><\/a> Part 2<\/span><\/a><\/p>\n

Politycs – Political Analysis<\/span><\/a><\/p>\n

Digging Deep into The Election Results of 2018<\/span><\/a><\/p>\n

This is a paid internship opportunity and a stipend will be given to selected interns. <\/p>\n

What we\u2019re looking for in you!<\/strong><\/p>\n

Now, being really into the data scene is good enough to qualify to apply for our internship program, but here\u2019s just a couple of things that would count as a really strong plus. <\/p>\n

– Beginner experience in working with either of R, Python, NodeJS<\/p>\n

– Experience with Javascript, AngularJS, React<\/p>\n

– Interest in learning about data analysis driven by Stats andML<\/p>\n

How to Apply<\/strong><\/p>\n

Send us a brief message over at careers@marketlytics.com along with your CV and an introduction of your unique experience and what makes you a good fit for the role. <\/p>\n
