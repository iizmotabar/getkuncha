About MarketLytics<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

MarketLytics is a user data consultancy and advanced analytics provider, that helps clients understand their user behavior. We collaborate with a network of clients and partners across 26 countries including F500s, fast-growing startups, and global e-commerce brands.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Job brief<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

We are looking for Digital Analysts who are passionate about analytics, reporting, and data analysis. The successful candidates will help build systems to collect information about customers, work with clients to understand their needs, analyze data and create insightful reports to help clients run their businesses better.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Qualifications and Experience<\/strong>
    • Bachelors in any field: Business, Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics or similar.<\/li>
    • Analytical and problem-solving skills<\/li>
    • Communication skills, verbal and in writing.<\/li>
    • Ability to meet deadlines, manage varied tasks and work with multiple teams.Skills that will be really cool to have (but not an issue if you don\u2019t):<\/strong><\/em> Having any of the following skills will be a plus<\/li><\/ul>
      • Knowledge and experience with online marketing tools.<\/li>
      • Prior Experience with tools like Google Analytics \/ Google Tag Manager is a plus<\/li>
      • Prior experience with Development (Javascript\/ Python) a strong plus<\/li>
      • Prior experience in SEM\/ SEO is a plus<\/li><\/ul><\/li>
      • Key Responsibilities<\/strong>
        • Help design and configure data collection systems for mobile and web (for example google analytics, mixpanel)<\/li>
        • Work with developers in implementing requirements for data collection<\/li>
        • Measure and report on digital marketing and client information datasets.<\/li>
        • Provide support to product owners in collecting, analyzing, and reporting on various user behavior (acquisition and engagement), and campaign effectiveness, to measure performance and support business decisions.<\/li>
        • Create data visualizations for performance reporting dashboards that allow users to quickly understand trends and make better decisions or drill down for further analysis.<\/li>
        • Communicate analysis with a focus on helping make better, faster decisions.
          <\/li><\/ul><\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
