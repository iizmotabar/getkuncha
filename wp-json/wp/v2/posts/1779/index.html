Google Analytics can not be only used for tracking website visitors but it is also used to track your email campaigns. In this guide, I will walk you through how to track Mailchimp Campaigns in Google Analytics.<\/p>\n

Create Your Mailchimp Campaign<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Login to your Mailchimp account
\nClick on campaigns tab and then click \u201ccreate campaign\u201d button.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Name your email campaign<\/p>\n

Select the subscribers list which you want to target<\/p>\n

On the setup page, scroll down to see the \u201cGoogle Analytics Link tracking Checkbox\u201d and check this box
\nEnter a unique title for the campaign in the title field.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Fill the rest of the steps and run the campaigns.<\/p>\n

View Campaign Reports in Google Analytics<\/strong><\/h2>\n

Once you run mailchimp campaign, it\u2019s time to view reports in Google Analytics.
\nLogin to your GA account and navigate to Acquisition >> Campaigns >> All Campaigns<\/strong><\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Under the campaigns list you will see the name of your email campaign that you entered in the above step.<\/p>\n
